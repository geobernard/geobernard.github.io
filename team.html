Our Team

Undergraduate Students, Graduate Students and Faculty

DiGTL is a collaborative effort that brings together undergraduate students, graduate students, and educators. We develop solutions and provide a unique learning experience that combines the expertise and skills of students and faculty from diverse disciplines to integrate state-of-the-art geospatial technologies in support of teaching, learning and research in the geosciences and other fields.
